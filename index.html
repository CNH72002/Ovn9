Bish-Bosh 2.0

Section 1: Printing all numbers from 1 to 100 that are either Bish or Bosh or both.

Here, we list all the numbers from 1 to 100 that are divisible by 3 as "bish" and those divisible by 4 as "bosh". Numbers divisible by 3 and 4 are represented as "bish-bosh". If any number is neither divisible by 3 nor divisible by 4, the number is listed with its digit instead.
